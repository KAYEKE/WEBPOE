About Green Cape Town

Empowering communities through environmental action since 2025

Our Story

Green Cape Town was founded in early 2025 by KAYLC a passionate student who recognized the urgent need for environmental action in our community. What started as a small tree-planting project in a local park has grown into a comprehensive sustainability initiative.

Our founders, inspired by global climate movements and local environmental challenges, decided to create practical solutions that engage communities directly. We believe that environmental change starts at the grassroots level, with individuals and communities working together.

Today, we've planted over 500 trees, engaged with 15 schools, and mobilized hundreds of volunteers. But we're just getting started.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To create a greener, more sustainable Cape Town through community-led environmental initiatives, education, and practical action. We empower individuals to become environmental stewards and create lasting positive change.

Our Vision

A Cape Town where every community actively participates in environmental conservation, where green spaces thrive, waste is minimized, and future generations inherit a healthy, sustainable environment.

Our Values

Community: We work with, not for, communities
Action: We believe in practical, measurable impact
Education: Knowledge empowers change
Sustainability: Long-term thinking guides our work
Inclusivity: Everyone has a role in environmental action
